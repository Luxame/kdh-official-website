Press & Recent Events

JARVISH Closes $1M Seed Financing, Led by Hazel Asset
Management and Vectr Ventures

TAIPEI, TAIWAN – (Jul 10, 2015) –

JARVISH Inc., designer of intelligent vehicle systems, has completed an $1 million seed round financing led by Hazel Asset Management, Vectr Ventures and selected angel investors. Proceeds from this financing will fund development of JARVISH’s advanced intelligent vehicle systems. JARVISH intends to use this financing to bring top industry technologists to join in its vision for a safer and enjoyable riding and driving experiences through Augmented Reality.

About Vectr Ventures

Vectr Ventures is an early-stage fund and studio based in Hong Kong. Vectr back teams with a global mindset, passion for product, critical thinking and creative problem solving. Vectr leads investments from Seed to Series A and syndicate with a network of regional and global strategic partners. Vectr works to be a globally trusted incubator and VC partner facilitating the creation and expansion of the world’s most disruptive technological innovations.

Our Services
JARVISH offer a Full Service digital helmet design. We applied the best-in-industry design methodology using the latest in Computer Aided Design software, and 3D prototyping technology to make sure our design fits into different types of motorcycle helmet.
JARVISH owns an in-house optic design team able to make different HUD design at different positions and angles inside the helmet and project focused display images to the motorcyclist.
JARVISH sensors collect data from the motorcycle and parts e.g. tires, and the road & weather conditions to generate calculated warning signals to motorcyclist which to prevent accidents at slippery road. The accident caused by the road condition accounts approximately 67% of all traffic accidents in recent years.
Using the gestural interface and voice control, an intuitive UI displays the supportive information onthe HUD and assist the motorcyclist to play music, answer phone calls and watch front or rear camera views anytime.
JARVISH Testing Procedure provides our helmet design a reliable testing process while complying to the latest helmet safety regulations, and electronic certification tests.
JARVISH will connect the motorcyclists and car drivers to the cloud and manage to calculate data generated from the helmet devices and camera images in real time. All contents created will become a real asset for safe drive with useful data, and also for security control.
